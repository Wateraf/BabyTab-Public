# BabyTab Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 10, 2026*

BabyTab ("the app") is a local-first baby care logger for parents and caregivers. This policy describes what data the app handles and how it is used.

**Developer:** Mizu Lab (see [SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md) for contact).

## Summary

- **We do not sell your data** or use it for advertising.
- **We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.**
- **Care logs** are stored **on your iPhone only** using SwiftData. We do not run servers that receive your baby data.
- **No account required.** There is no Sign in with Apple in the current version.
- **Not medical advice.** Visit Summary and other outputs are for personal record-keeping — not diagnosis or treatment.

## Information you enter

BabyTab stores the information you choose to record, such as:

- Baby name and optional birth date
- Care events (feeds, diapers, sleep, solids, custom logs)
- Timer sessions and optional event details (amounts, directions, notes)
- Diary notes
- Button and appearance preferences

This data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## Data on your device only

We do not collect, transmit, or store your care logs on our servers. We cannot read your records.

The app works offline. Network access is not required for core logging features.

## Widget and Live Activity

If you add the BabyTab widget or use timer Live Activities, the app extension reads the same on-device data through an Apple App Group container (`group.mizulab.BabyTab`). This sharing stays on your device and is used only to display your dashboard or active timers.

## Export and import

You may export your data as **CSV** or **Visit Summary PDF** through the app. Exports are created on your device and shared only when you choose (AirDrop, Mail, Files, etc.).

CSV import reads a file you select and writes matching records to local storage. Import never sends data to us.

## Apple services

BabyTab is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may process purchase, device, and crash information according to [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not receive your care log content through the App Store.

## Children's information

BabyTab is intended for parents and caregivers to log information about their baby. The app is not directed at children to use on their own. Information about a baby is entered and controlled by the device owner.

## Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, delete the app, or erase your device. Uninstalling BabyTab removes local app data subject to iOS behavior and backups.

## Security

Your data benefits from your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, encryption). You are responsible for securing your device and any exported files you create.

## Your choices

- Use the app without creating any online account.
- Export CSV before reinstalling or switching devices.
- Deny optional permissions if added in future versions.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The **last updated** date at the top will change; the current version is always in this repository.
