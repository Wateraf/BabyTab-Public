## Common topics

**Lost data / new phone**  
Care logs are saved **on your iPhone**. Before reinstalling or switching devices, use **Settings → Data → Export CSV** and keep the file safe. On the new phone, install BabyTab, then **Import CSV**.

Without an export, deleting the app removes your saved records. An **iCloud or computer device backup** of your iPhone may also preserve app data, but the in-app CSV export is the reliable way to move logs.

**Widget or Live Activity not updating**  
Make sure the widget is added from the home screen and that BabyTab has been opened at least once after logging. Timer Live Activities appear when you start a timer with a double tap.

**Visit Summary PDF**  
Open **Calendar → Visit Summary** to generate a PDF for pediatric visits. This is for personal record-keeping — not medical advice.

**Gestures**  
- Press & release — log instantly  
- Press & drag — add detail (wet/dirty, side, oz)  
- Double tap — start / stop timer  

See in-app **Settings → Help → Gestures** for more.
